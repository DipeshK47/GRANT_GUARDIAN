# 2024 Annual Program Outcomes Report

Organization: Bridgelight Youth Foundation
Program: Academic Acceleration Program
Reporting Period: January 2024 - December 2024

## Headline outcomes

- 312 students participated in direct services during 2024.
- 84% of program participants graduated from high school.
- The comparable district average was 61%.
- 91% of graduating seniors were accepted into a post-secondary program.
- Average GPA improvement across the tutoring cohort was 0.6 points.
- 280 families received family navigation support.
- 88% of surveyed caregivers reported improved confidence supporting attendance and academic planning.
